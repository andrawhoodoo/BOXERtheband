ABOUT   US

Our Story

BOXER is an Indie/Pop Rock band out of East Rockaway NY. The Band Consists of Lenny Sadowsky, Andrew Friedman, Dave Wolfson, and Johnny Pots. BOXER was formed in early 2017 and came to be due to a chance meeting at The Cup in Wantagh, NY. The name BOXER is to honor a close friend who the band lost, Gail Boxer.
