ABOUT   US

Boxer is recently formed band made by chance on one fateful open mic at The Cup in Wantagh, NY. The band's name, Boxer, is named after a beloved friend that was taken from us too soon named, Gail Boxer. Gail throughout her life was a collector and lover of all forms of art. She was so classic, but at the same time a trend setter. This page is not only an expression of our bands work, but also her art as well. Both will be featured on this page. We simply do this for our mutual love of our crafts, passions, and expressions of our true selves, and most importantly, for the art of yesterday, today, and tomorrow.
